# Codes and Nomenclators

## Mathematical Definition

A codebook mapping $\mathcal{W} \rightarrow \mathcal{C}$ from words/phrases to numeric or symbolic codewords:

$$E(w_1 w_2 \ldots w_n) = c_1 \; c_2 \; \ldots \; c_n, \quad c_i = \text{Codebook}(w_i)$$
